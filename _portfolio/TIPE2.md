This project was conducted as part of my TIPE (Research Project) for the 2024–2025 academic year.

### Objective
The goal of this project was to explore the intersection between neuroscience and music. I aimed to translate biological signals (specifically brain waves) into audible melodies in real-time.

### Technical Realization
* **Hardware:** Designed and developed a custom EEG device in the laboratory to record brain activity.
* **Software:** Used **Pure Data** (Pd) to process the raw EEG signals and map them to musical parameters (pitch, rhythm, and timbre) to generate coherent melodies.

### Skills Developed
* Signal processing
* Electronic circuit design
* Programming with Pure Data
